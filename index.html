Services

Telehealth counseling across North Carolina. Ages 10+.

Individual Therapy for Men

For men who feel like they have to carry everything and still stay composed. We will work on stress, anger, emotional shutdown, and the patterns that keep you stuck. Sessions are direct, supportive, and focused on change you can actually feel.

  • Stress, anger, and emotional suppression
  • Grief and loss
  • Trauma and coping patterns
  • Confidence, discipline, and direction

Couples Therapy

For couples who are tired of repeating the same argument or feeling emotionally disconnected. We focus on communication, conflict patterns, trust repair, and rebuilding a sense of safety and teamwork.

  • Communication and conflict
  • Emotional disconnection
  • Repair after betrayal
  • Premarital counseling

Adolescent Therapy (10+)

For adolescents ages 10+ who are dealing with anxiety, emotional outbursts, school stress, or family tension. Work is structured and age-appropriate, and I collaborate with caregivers when helpful.

  • Anxiety and emotional regulation
  • School stress and peer conflict
  • Family communication
  • Self-esteem and identity development

My approach

My style is straightforward, respectful, and structured. I listen, but I also help you identify patterns, challenge what is not serving you, and build skills you can use outside of session.

You can expect clarity, accountability, and support. The goal is not to become someone else. The goal is to function better, communicate better, and feel more steady in your life and relationships.

Telehealth model

All sessions are conducted online. You can join from any private location in North Carolina.

  • No commute
  • Increased privacy
  • Flexible scheduling

You will need a stable internet connection and a private space.
